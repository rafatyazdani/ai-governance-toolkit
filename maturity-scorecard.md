# AI Governance Maturity Scorecard

> Score your organization's AI governance program across five domains. Use results to prioritize investments and communicate maturity to leadership.

---

## Scoring Guide

Rate each capability on a 1–4 scale:

| Score | Level | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Initial | Ad hoc or absent. No formal process. Outcomes unpredictable. |
| 2 | Developing | Basic process exists but inconsistently applied. Dependent on individuals. |
| 3 | Defined | Documented, consistently applied, and understood organization-wide. |
| 4 | Optimized | Measured, continuously improved, and benchmarked against industry. |

## Maturity Level Interpretation

| Total Score | Maturity Level | What it means |
|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| 80–100 | **Optimized** | Industry-leading AI governance. Focus on benchmarking and continuous improvement. |
| 60–79 | **Defined** | Solid foundation. Prioritize consistency and measurement. |
| 40–59 | **Developing** | Core elements emerging. Focus on formalizing the highest-risk areas first. |
| 20–39 | **Initial** | Governance largely ad hoc. Immediate investment required in ownership, policy, and high-risk controls. |
| <20 | **Absent** | No meaningful AI governance program. Significant regulatory and reputational exposure. |

---

## Recommended Priorities by Score Band

**If score is 20–39 (Initial):**
- Assign AI risk ownership immediately
- Draft and approve AI use policy
- Complete AI use case inventory
- Implement intake/approval process for new AI

**If score is 40–59 (Developing):**
- Risk-tier all use cases
- Implement pre-deployment testing for high/critical tier
- Stand up basic model monitoring
- Conduct AI security assessment

**If score is 60–79 (Defined):**
- Formalize metrics and board reporting
- Implement bias testing program
- Formalize vendor AI DDQ process
- Conduct regulatory gap assessment (EU AI Act, ISO 42001)

**If score is 80–100 (Optimized):**
- Pursue ISO 42001 certification
- Benchmark against peers
- Publish transparency report
- Contribute to industry standards
